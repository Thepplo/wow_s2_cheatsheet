First Boss

Tussle Tonks

This boss has seen a substantial rework since it's debut in BFA, they now need to be killed at the same time and have new mechanics.
The arena, like before, will periodically spawn sawblades that need to be dodged. The blades also knock you back which can combo you with another mechanic.
There are also 4 hammers scattered throughout the arena's edge which will play an important role for both bosses.

G.4.U.:

Foe Flipper: Targets a player, and after a brief duration, will deal damage to them and knock them back.
B.4.T.T.L.3. Mine: Spawns multiple mines at the locations of the players, this is the ability that can combo with knockbacks, be careful of landing on a mine if you know you will be knocked back. .
Maximum Thrust: Targets a random player, the tank will, after drawing a frontal line, drive towards that player quickly, dodge this.

The Platinum Pummeler:

Platinum Pummel: Tankbuster that does aoe damage in a circle around tank.
Ground Pound: Multiple instances of unavoidable AoE damage.
Platinum Plating: A personal buff of armor that needs to be removed by kiting him into a hammer.
When a hammer has hit the boss it will deactivate rendering it unusable.

This is a section about the Maximum Thrust/Platinum Plating overlap.
These 2 casts overlap, so if you want to minmax you can bait G.4.U.: to charge into the hammer that will remove Platinum Plating:.
If you pull this off both bosses will be hit by the hammer and stunned at the same location allowing for big damage.

Second Boss

K.U.J.O

The main mechanics here are basically unchanged from BFA.
Blazing Chomp: A large tank-hit (that he will do immediately when pulled) that leaves a DoT magic dispel . When Dispelled it will do AoE damage to the party.
Explosive Leap: Boss draws circles around players in an order, after a short duration he will leap to them in order doing heavy damage, don't stack too close.
Air Drop: Throughout the fight boxes are dropped close to players into the arena that provide LoS, boxes take "damage" from Explosive Leap and Venting Flames:, and they will be destroyed when they take too much damage.
Venting Flames: Deals deadly amount of AoE to everyone if they are not in LoS behind a box. If you hide behind a damaged box it will disappear during venting flames the box will be destroyed before the final pulse of damage and you will die.

Before Third Boss

Notable Mobs:

Mechagon Tinkerer:

Caster with a dangerous cast.
Giga Wallop: Targets random player with single-target nuke, can be interrupted.

D.B.M III:

Scary mob, spawns with 2 nerfed versions of itself.
Arcing Zap: A cast on a random player that puts a DoT magic dispel on them. Short Out: A channel of unavoidable AoE damage, very dangerous for people who have debuffs.

Blastatron X-80:

Scary immobile mob. During it's casts it deals periodic AoE-damage.
Sonic Pulse: A cast on a random player, draws a frontal line towards them, dodge this.
Capacitor Discharge:Spawns 3 quadrants of frontals from it with one slice/section being safe. Repeats this a couple of times.

Defender:

Shield Generator: Spawns a bubble on itself, everything standing in it gets a DR-buff, including us. Kite the mobs out of it.

Mechanic:

Tune up: Attempts to heal a mech that is low on hp, this cast can be cc'd.

Third Boss

Machinists Garden

Boss is the middle of a circle shaped arena with a big pillar in the middle. The boss constantly spawns sawblades that bounce around the room which deal damage to anyone it comes in contact with. These never despawn. Activate Plant: Spawns an add that spams random players with single-target damage, nuke this add down. The add can also be cc'd
Activate "Hidden" Flame Turrets:Activates the three flame turrets on the pillar that have an arena long flame line. These spin in a random direction.
As the boss casts this you can see the turrets winding up and preemptively dodge them.
