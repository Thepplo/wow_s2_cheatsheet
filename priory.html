The Priory of Sacred Flame

Before First Boss

Notable Mobs:

Sharpshooter:

Very dangerous mob, cannot be positioned easily due to cc immunity.
Shoot: Shoots the tank.
Pot Shot: Snipes a random target for heavy single target damage.
Caltrops: Jumps closer to the party and puts down 3 bear traps.

Arathi Knight:

Dangerous mob. Especially in combination with Sharpshooters
Impale: Single target bleed on random player.
Disrupting Shout: AoE damage with an interrupting effect.

Guard Captain Suleyman

First miniboss.
Shield Slam: VERY heavy tankbuster, knocks back. There is a spot near the fountain between the first and second steps that negate this knockback.
Thunderclap:AOE damage that then slows everyone.
After death, attracts Sargeant Shaynemail from the first boss.

Sargeant Shaynemail:

First miniboss that comes from the first boss.
Lunging Strike: Jumps to one player, dealing heavy single target damage and bleeds them.
Brutal Smash: Standard circle of death around him, careful of backing into nearby packs.

War lynx:

Powerful mob.
Pounce: Jumps to one player, dealing single target damage and bleeds them.

Fanatical Conjurer:

Powerful caster.
Fireball: Standard bolt that it spams.
Flamestrike: Puts flame patches on the ground.

Lightspawn:

VERY dangerous little rat bastard of a mob, akin to Corruptors in Grim Batol. WITH an ability that can heal other mobs.
Purification: Channels single target damage unto random player for 5s.
Burst of Light: When on low health the Lightspawn will cast a circle around it, players in the circle die, other mobs get healed. ETA gg if other mobs are casting in circle.

High Priest Aemya

Second miniboss.
Holy Smite: Targets random player, casts big smite. Can be kicked.
Reflective Shield:Puts a buff on herself that absorbs and reflects damage done to her.
After death, attracts Taener Duelmal from the first boss.

Taener Duelmal

Second miniboss from the first boss.
Cinder Blast: Targets tank with massive ST-nuke. Must be kicked.
Fireball:Targets random player with ST-nuke. Can be kicked.
Reflective Shield:Puts a buff on herself that absorbs and reflects damage done to her.

Forge Master Damian

Third miniboss that you pull if you go right instead of left.
Flame Seal: Buffs himself to do more tank-damage. Spams swirlies while buffed.
Heat wave:Massive AoE-nuke.
After death, attracts Elaena Emberlanz from the first boss.

Elaena Emberlanz

Third miniboss from the first boss.
Divine Judgement: Tankbuster.
Holy Radiance:Pulses AoE damage.

First Boss

Commander Dailcry

This boss will always have a partner, either Elaena Emberlanz or Taener Duelmal depending on what route was taken.

When Dailcry dies, the partner also dies so no need to kill both, can just go full ST on Dailcry.

Pierce Armor: Tankbuster that leaves a vulnerability debuff, this combos into Elaena Emberlanz's Divine Judgement if present.

Hurl Spear: Line frontal that can be side-stepped.

Savage mauling:Jumps to random DPS and spams damage on them, need to break the shield to interrupt him. He casts Battle Cry after that needs to be kicked.
If Taener Duelmal is present you will need to kick his casts too which can be hard in a pug-setting.

Before Second Boss

Notable Mobs:

This area is very confined with alot of deadly mobs. When the room is cleared, more packs of Templars, Paladins and Priests come down the stairs.

Argent Paladin:

Very dangerous mob.
Consecration: Puts down a large consecration on the floor.
Sacred toll: Consecutive AOE damage nuke on everyone, this ability usually combos into the other Argent Paladin's Sacred toll.

Zealous Templar:

Mob with ALOT of tank damage.There are alot of them here so tank will likely get demolished.
Buffs:Constantly gets random paladin buffs that buff it's tank damage.

Second Boss

Baron Braunpyke

It's a good idea for the party to stack on one side of the room at the beginning here to bait swirlies.

Hammer of Purity: Spawns 3 swirlies at 3 player's locations. These explode into swirling hammers that expand outwards, this is why it's a good idea to stack and then to move to the other side.

Sacrificial Pyre: Spawns a circle with a number of orbs around it. The circle needs to be soaked a number of times according to the number of orbs. Soaking an orb leaves a DoT. Every time an orb is soaked AoE damage goes onto the group. If Sacrificial Pyre is not soaked, it's a wipe.

Burning Light:Interruptable cast that deals AoE damage and heals the boss if it goes through.

Vindictive Wrath: Boss pops wings and buffs his other abilities, i.e. more swirlies, more pyre soaks and circles around boss that need to be avoided..

Before Final Boss

Most of the mobs here are recycled from previous areas but there's a few new ones.

Notable Mobs:

Risen Mage:

Caster with one dangerous cast.
Fireball Volley AoE damage nuke if not kicked.

Sir Braunpyke:

Very dangerous miniboss that you probably don't want to pull more packs on. There is a patrol around the room so pulling Braunpyke back to the entrance is a good idea.
Aura: Braunpyke has an aura that pulses constant rot damage to the entire group, this is why pulling casters (which there are alot of here) probably isn't a good idea.
Blazing Strike: A very heavy tankbuster. Make sure to mitigate this to help your healer, as they are probably busy with the rot damage.
Killing Braunpyke is neccesary to open area to last boss.

Final Boss

Prioress Murpray

When pulled will cover area with swirlies that create dangerous puddles

Holy Smite: Targets a player with a large damage nuke. This can be interrupted so having an interrupt order here is key.

Blinding Light: A "lookaway" mechanic, if you are facing the boss when it finishes you are disoriented.

Holy Flame: Spawns a circle on a player, move out of it.

Purify: Targets the player with a beam that must be kited, beam will leave puddles of light on the ground, try to aim these puddles at the edges of the room.

At 50% the boss will transition, jumping up to the top of the platform and starting to cast,

Embrace the Light: Pulses aoe damage, will be interrupted when the boss' shield breaks.

While she is channeling, the boss will spawn adds at the bottom of the stairs, these will melee whoever they want if they are in range, it appears that tanking these is very hard.
To counteract this, all players can stack at a point by the fence so that all adds group up and come up the stairs from a single direction.
