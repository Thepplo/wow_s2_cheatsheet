The Priory of Sacred Flame

Before First Boss

Notable Mobs:

Sharpshooter:

Very dangerous mob, cannot be positioned easily due to cc immunity.
Shoot: Shoots the tank.
Pot Shot: Snipes a random target for heavy single target damage.
Caltrops: Jumps closer to the party and puts down 3 bear traps.

Arathi Knight:

Dangerous mob. Especially in combination with Sharpshooters
Impale: Single target bleed on random player.
Disrupting Shout: AoE damage with an interrupting effect.

Guard Captain Suleyman

First miniboss.
Shield Slam: VERY heavy tankbuster, knocks back. There is a spot near the fountain between the first and second steps that negate this knockback.
Thunderclap:AOE damage that then slows everyone.
After death, attracts another miniboss from the first boss.

Sargeant Shaynemail:

First miniboss that comes from the first boss.
Lunging Strike: Jumps to one player, dealing heavy single target damage and bleeds them.
Brutal Smash: Standard circle of death around him, careful of backing into nearby packs.

War lynx:

Powerful mob.
Pounce: Jumps to one player, dealing single target damage and bleeds them.

Fanatical Conjurer:

Powerful caster.
Fireball: Standard bolt that it spams.
Flamestrike: Puts flame patches on the ground.

Lightspawn:

VERY dangerous little rat bastard of a mob, akin to Corruptors in Grim Batol. WITH an ability that can heal other mobs.
Purification: Channels single target damage unto random player for 5s.
Burst of Light: When on low health the Lightspawn will cast a circle around it, players in the circle die, other mobs get healed. ETA gg if other mobs are casting in circle.

High Priest Aemya

Second miniboss.
Holy Smite: Targets random player, casts big smite. Can be kicked.
Reflective Shield:Puts a buff on herself that absorbs and reflects damage done to her.
After death, attracts Taener Duelmal from the first boss.

Taener Duelmal

Second miniboss from the first boss..
Cinder Blast: Targets tank with massive ST-nuke. Must be kicked.
Fireball:Targets random player with ST-nuke. Can be kicked.
Reflective Shield:Puts a buff on herself that absorbs and reflects damage done to her.

Forge Master Damian

Third miniboss that you pull if you go right instead of left..
Flame Seal: Buffs himself to do more tank-damage. Spams swirlies while buffed.
Heat wave:Massive AoE-nuke.
After death, attracts Elaena Emberlanz from the first boss.

Forge Master Damian

Third miniboss from the first boss.
Divine Judgement: Tankbuster.
Holy Radiance:Pulses AoE damage.

First Boss

Commander Dailcry

This boss will always have a partner, either Elaena Emberlanz or Taener Duelmal depending on what route was taken.

Pierce Armor: Tankbuster that leaves a vulnerability debuff, this combos into Elaena Emberlanz's Divine Judgement if present.

Hurl Spear: Line frontal that can be side-stepped.

Savage mauling:Jumps to random DPS and spams damage on them, need to break the shield to interrupt him. He casts Battle Cry after that needs to be kicked.
If Taener Duelmal is present you will need to kick his casts too which can be hard in a pug-setting.

Before Second Boss

Notable Mobs:

Azerite Extractor:

Don't pull 2 of these at the same time
Puncture: Strong tankbuster
Rapid Extraction: Pulses AOE damage and spawns swirlies.

Earthshaper:

Strong caster
Earthlance: Single target nuke, must be kicked.

Stonefury:

Another strong caster with a shield cast that doesn't do a lot but has,
Furious Quake: Huge AOE damage nuke, needs to be kicked.

Taskmaster Askari:

Very terrifying miniboss, constantly buffs mobs around him and puts bleed on tank.
Charge: Charges and nukes one player then swirlies around him

Second Boss

Azerokk

Spawns two Earthragers upon pull that fixate onto random players, tries to bleed them. Kite these.

Azerite infusion: Empowers one of the ragers, can't be CC'd now.

Tectonic Slam: Frontal.

Resonant Quake: AOE damage that leaves slowing zone that can be used when kiting adds.

Call Earthragers: Summons more Earthragers.

Before Third Boss

Notable Mobs:

V.C. Alchemist:

Caster with one dangerous cast.
Transmute: Enemy to goo: Targets one player and polymorphs them if not kicked.

Mastermind:

Mob with uninterruptable single target damage and,
Brainstorm: Spawns moving swirlies that silence.

Tester:

Echo Blade: Sends out projectile to all players, need to spread out so they dont echo on eachother.

Third Boss

Rixxa Fluxflame

When pulled will cover area with swirlies that create dangerous puddles

Azerite Catalyst: Targets a player that will spawn more swirlies that leave azerite, bait them around the existing puddles

Propellent Blast: Targets a player and will blow them away like with a leafblower, bait this towards puddles as this pushes puddles back too

Boss will refill the room after 3Propellent Blasts

Before final boss

Notable Mobs:

V.O.W Machine:

Big dangerous mech
Charged shot:Pulsing aoe damage channel
Deploy Crawler Mine:Deploys a mine that fixates someone, if you walk into it you die. Can be cc'd so help your buddies.

Final Boss

Mogul Razdonk

Mogul will spawn 2 Boom bots that will go to two seperate edges of the room. Depending on where they are facing (either horizontally or vertically) they will draw a line of swirlies.
Think of them like the dragons on the first boss of Grim Batol. ALWAYS watch where they are and where the swirlies will be. You can't really dodge the swirlies on reaction.

Homing Missile: Targets one player with a homing missile with a large circle of impact area, kite this out.
Gatling Gun : Targets the tank with a black cone, this cone will then spin around like a gatling gun. Tank faces this away from the group.

INTERMISSION

During intermission the boss will spawn two snipers that constantly shoot at random players. Mark one and burst it down as this does heavy damage.
Drill smash: Targets one player with an arrow, there are pillars that spawn around the room, targeted player baits it on the pillar. When he lands on a pillar he does ALOT of aoe damage. After Mogul smashed 3 pillars the intermission will end.
