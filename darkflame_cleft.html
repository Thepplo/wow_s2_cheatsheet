Before First Boss

Notable Mobs:

Rank Overseer:

Big kobold dude whose abilities impact the players and other mobs.
Wild Wallpo: Big AoE around him that will damage + knockback players AND other mobs.
Overpowering Roar: Unavoidable AoE damage on all players.

Wicklighter:

Dangerous caster
Wicklighter bolt: Single target bolt, can be kicked

Moleherd:

Has potentially dangerous cast.
Mole Frenzy: Enrages all mobs in vicinity, can and should be kicked.
Throw Rock: Spams this cast of single target damage on random player. Can be very dangerous in combination with Overpowering Roar overlaps.

Mole Laborer:

Stacking tank-damage.
Debuff: Builds a stacking debuff on tank on melees.

Ol' Waxbeard

Ol' Waxbeard

This Arena features 4 train tracks in a cross formation. When a track lights up orange it means a train cart will be traveling down the track soon.
If you get run-over by the train cart you die. But the train carts also do friendly fire to the adds that will spawn.
Spawning Adds: Throughout the fight Mole Laborers will spawn that run around and hit players.
They can be cc'd and they can be baited into traveling train carts to knock them back/kill them.
Luring Candleflame: Ol' Waxbeard targets a random player and commands all Mole Laborers to fixate on them. Kite/cc/bait these mobs.
Underhanded Track-ticts: Spawns a stationary killable train cart that needs to be focused down before it explodes.
Rock Charge: Charges a random player, try to bait this into pillars so the boss doesn't charge away too far.

Blazikon

Blazikon

Big stationary boss in a hexagonal arena. There are candlesby the edges of the arena that are vital to the fight.

Dousing Breath: Huge unavoidable AoE damage. Also douses all candles.
Wicklighter Barrage: Puts a marker on 3 people who will periodically over a few seconds leave fire swirlies where they stand.
These circles light all candles within the circles. Place these circles by candles.
Enkindling Inferno: A conal frontal towards all unlit candles. Lit candles do not have frontals towards them so stand by them to dodge.
If all candles are doused it's a wipe.
This will also do big unavoidable AoE damage that needs to be healed.
Extinguishing Gusty: Marks 3 people and after a brief delay sends wind tornados towards them. These gusts douses unlit candles in their path.
Bait these to atleast one candle for the next mechanic. Incite Flames: Spawns flame patches on all slices of the room that have lit candles.
The areas with unlit candles won't have flame patches so stand there to avoid.
If all candles are lit it's a wipe.

Before Third Boss

Notable Mobs:

Flame tender:

Caster with one bolt cast.
Flame bolt: Targets random player, is kickable.

Torchsnarl/Sootsnout:

2 minibosses that do overlap mechanics.
One hand headlock/Ceaseless fire: Sootsnout grabs a targeted player, holding them in place. Torchsnarl channels a fire frontal on them while they are held in place.
The headlock target might be countered by invis.
Massive stomp: Sootsnout stomps, doing big unavoidable AoE damage. This is cast directly after the headlock/fire so can be very dangerous.
Flaming Tether/Pyro Pummel. Torchsnarl targets a player with a kickable cast that roots them. Sootsnout will dash to the targeted player doing multiple hits with a fire frontal.
Kick the tether and dodge the frontals.

The Candle King

The Candle King

This boss is all about managing the wax statues he spawns, trying to destroy as many as you can with his other mechanics.
Eerie Molds: Spawns a wax statues by each player, try to stack loosely to make it easier to destroy.
These wax statues pulse unavoidable AoE damage to all players while up. Paranoid Mind: Fears everyone if cast goes through, must be kicked. Darkflame Pickaxe: Targets one player with a pickaxe he will throw towards them after a delay indicated by an arrow.
This pickaxe will destroy any wax statues between the targeted player and the boss.
Try to aim this ability at any statues that aren't grouped with the rest of them since the next mechanic will destroy in an area.
Throw Darkflame: Marks 3 people with a circle that will do unavoidable damage after a brief delay.
Any wax statues inside the circles when they time out will melt leaving a growing pool of wax underneath them.
Walking into these puddles will stun you.
It seems like the previous wax pools will reset after each Throw Darkflame cast.

Before final boss

Notable Mobs:

After the third boss you will start the minecart event. This involves standing near a minecart with a candle that provides light which is necessary to heal/dps the mobs on the way.
The candle will slowly burn out, to fill it up again you need to collect candles on the way and bring it to the cart. A ranged player can tag mobs to pull them towards the cart.

Shuffling Darkness:

Mob who can drain the candle with it's cast.
Drain light:A kickable cast that drains the light from the cart, kick this.

Corridor Creeper:

VERY dangerous mobs for tanks.
Stacking Debuff:As the Creeper melees it places a stacking vulnerability debuff on the tank.
Stacking Buff:When the Creeper is near corpses of other mobs it gets a stacking damage buff for each corpse..
Move the creeper AWAY from corpses.

The Darkness

The Darkness

The Fight "starts" with an intermission where you ride a fast minecart while the Darkness chases you. While on this ride you can throw Dynamite on The Darkness to damage it.
The damage you do here carries over to when you actually pull him later and start the real fight.

PULLING THE BOSS

Candle: On the actual fight you have an interactible stationary candle like the minecart from before.
This candle provides light and can be picked up by anyone with the extra action button after a short cast. When picked up you can move it.
To refuel the candle you need to go out and pick up candles by the ends of the arena and bring it back to the main candle like in the minecart event.
If the candle is drained fully the boss will fear you.
Shadow Blast : Targets one player with a large circle that will do damage after a brief delay, move this away from your friends and especially the candle as this will drain it.
Umbral slash: Targets the candle with an wide frontal. Pick it up and move it away from the frontal as this will drain it, dodge the frontal. Eternal Darkness: A very heavy unavoidable AoE damage channel that does 4 ticks of damage.
This also drains the candle heavily so try to have it topped before and try to have a candle on standby for the AoE.
Call Darkspawn: Summons adds that try to drain the candle with a kickable cast.
