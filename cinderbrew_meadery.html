Brew Master Aldryr

Brew Master Aldryr

This Arena is placed in a bar, there are alot of tables around that will be important during the intermission.
Keg Smash: Heavy tank-buster that knocks you back and leaves puddles on the ground.
Throw Cinderbrews: Throws cinderbrew at 2 random players infecting them with a heavy DoT-effect.
Blazing Belch: Cone frontal.
INTERMISSION 66%/33%: At 66% and 33% the boss will enter an intermission. The boss is invulnerable and stands behind the bar, occasionally spawning mugs of beer that can be picked up.
These beers need to be delivered to patrons that are sitting by the tables at the left and right sides of the arena. They glow red.
Happy Hour: During this intermission pulsing AoE-damage goes out onto the party.
When all the beers have been delivered the boss ends the intermission and repeats the first phase with new moving swirlies.

Berik Buzzbee

Blazikon

A potentially very chaotic fight that revolves around the adds the boss spawns so let's talk about those first.

Snack Time: The boss spawns Barrels around the arena.
These Barrels will continously spawn adds as long as they are up. The adds are called Raging Buzzbees. These adds will jump around and place DoTs on the party.
When killed these Raging Buzzbees will become Interactible. If you click on them you will Ride the bee, allowing you to use their ability to dash through Barrels, destroying them.
That is the main gimmick of this fight.
Honey Marinade: Puts a circle around the tank that will after a delay, do damage and drop a flame patch on the ground.
Try to position this around the edges of the arena. Fluttering Wing: A channel of unavoidable AoE damage that pushes everyone away slightly from the boss.
This becomes dangerous with the DoTs from the Raging Buzzbees.

I'pa

I'pa

This is a very tank/cc heavy boss, all about managing the adds the boss spawns which will need to be kited. Spouting Stout: Boss will spawn alot of swirlies, of which there are 2 kinds.
Yellow swirlies: which are normal swirlies.
And Red swirlies: which will spawn adds.
These Brew Drops will run towards the boss. If they reach the boss, it will give the boss a 10% shield and he will do heavy AoE damage.
The Brew Drops can be cc'd (roots,stuns,vortex,etc.) but it is recommended to kite these.
If they stay alive long they will become faster.
On death the Brew Drops will leave a puddle on the ground and slowly regenerate to spawn again.
Bottoms Uppercut: Heavy tank-buster that also knocks back.
Burning Fermentation: Applies a dispellable DoT magic debuff.

Coldie Baronbottom

Coldie Baronbottom

This can, again, become a very chaotic fight. It's all about managing the Big Barrels: she spawns.
Spread the Love!: The boss spawns alot of barrels around the room, of which there are 2 kinds,
4 Big Barrels and alot ofSmall barrels.
Both barrels, when destroyed, will spawn multiple lava waves from it that need to be dodged, but when a Big Barrel is destroyed it will apply a party wide STACKING DoT that refreshes on multiple applications. Akin to Skarmorak Shards from SV.
These barrels will have to be destroyed by players with 2 different mechanics from the boss before she explodes all of them at the same time causing a wipe.
Cash Cannon: Targets the tank with an wide frontal.
Aim this towards Barrels: to destroy them.
Burning Ricochet: Targets 2 random party members with a damaging circle which will explode after a brief delay, any Barrels: caught inside will ignite and explode.
Let it Hail: The boss channels HEAVY pusling AoE damage on everyone, whilst exploding any existing Barrels:.
So as you can see this boss becomes hectic without a clear strategy on how to explode the barrels before Let it Hail:, and timing it correctly so that the DoT effect from the Big Barrels: doesn't refresh/kill anyone.
What appears to be the leading strategy is to explode them in a 1-1-1-1 fashion, as you get 2 Cash Cannons and 2 Burning Ricochets per wave of barrels.
