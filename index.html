WoW Dungeon Boss Cheat Sheets

The Necrotic Wake

Blightbone: Dodge charges, kill adds fast.

Amarth: Interrupt Final Harvest, CC adds.

Surgeon Stitchflesh: Kite the hook into the boss.

Nalthor the Rimebinder: Avoid frozen ground, jump platforms.

Mists of Tirna Scithe

Ingra Maloch: DPS down the add to weaken boss.

Mistcaller: Avoid fox, match symbols in maze.

Tred'ova: Interrupt Mind Link, avoid ground AoEs.
