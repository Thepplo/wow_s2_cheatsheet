The Rookery

Before First Boss

Notable Mobs:

Quartermaster Koratite:

Dangerous miniboss
Bounding Void: Cone that launches 3 damaging boomerang circles, ranged can currently stand on platform before to avoid this entirely.
Entropy Shield:Creates a shield that deflects damage towards where it's facing. Go behind it to damage him.

Cursed thunderer:

Dangerous caster
Lightning bolt: Single target damage to random player, should be interrupted.

Void Rider:

Probably most dangerous mob here.
Wild Lightning: Creates cone of swirlies to dodge.
Localized Storm: Pulses heavy AOE damage. These Riders are most often paired withCursed thunderers so this damage combined with bolts can be dangerous

First Boss

Kyrioss

Crashing thunder: Everyone gets damaging circle, don't overlap these.

Wild lightning: Cone of swirlies to dodge.

Lightning Torrent: An intermission phase that happens at 100 energy. Boss teleports to middle of the room and gets 4 orbs around him.
The orbs turn into rotating beams. Find a safe spot between two orbs. Slowly pulses aoe damage when the beams are rotating. During intermission everyone gets circles under them periodically.

Before Second Boss

Notable Mobs:

Diffuser:

Dangerous aoe damage.
Attracting Shadows: Pulses aoe damage whilst trying to suck in players into death-zone around him, akin to last boss in Ara'Kara.
Arcing Void:Same as the cast in Stonevault.

Corrupted Oracle:

Strong caster
Void Bolt: Single target nuke, should be kicked.
Seeping Corruption: Target someone with a DoT curse , this can be decursed.

Crusher:

Strong anti-tank mob.
Debuff:Puts a stacking debuff on tank, if tank needs to drop stacks the crushers can be cc'd.
Void Crush:Targets a player and leaps to them doing damage, don't stand near targeted player as it does aoe.

Second Boss

Stormguard Corren

The main mechanic here is his ability Chaotic Corruption:
This is a hot potato 4s DoT debuff, meaning it spreads after it times out/gets dispelled. It spreads to the closest person and leaves the holder with a 6s vulnerability debuff. You always have to pass it to someone new.

Crush Reality: Targets someone with a big circle that Corren jumps to. When he lands, cyclones will spawn from the circle. This makes positioning with the debuff complicated.

Dark Gravity: Boss tries to suck in players while dealing ticking aoe damage. Both this and Crush Reality: leave puddles after them.

Before Final Boss

Notable Mobs:

Void Ascendant:

Caster with dangerous casts.
Void Bolt: Targets random player with bolt, can be interrupted.
Feasting Void: Does aoe damage to everyone and puts a heal absorb on everyone
Umbral Wave: Spawns 4 orbs around her that get sent out, she does this twice. Find a safe spot between them.

Corrupted Voidstone:

Very scary and weird dude.
Void Extraction: A cast that extracts void essence from everything around him, including us and all mobs around him.
All targets that get extracted get a damage penalty debuff and for every extracted target the Stone gets a stacking damage buff.
Unleashed Darkness:At 40% the stone casts this 18 second long ability. Pulsing aoe damage and spawning swirlies. This gets very fucky very fast with the damage amp/decreasing debuffs.

Tester:

Echo Blade: Sends out projectile to all players, need to spread out so they dont echo on eachother.

Final Boss

Voidstone Monstrosity

When pulled will cover area with swirlies that create dangerous puddles

Azerite Catalyst: Targets a player that will spawn more swirlies that leave azerite, bait them around the existing puddles

Propellent Blast: Targets a player and will blow them away like with a leafblower, bait this towards puddles as this pushes puddles back too

Boss will refill the room after 3Propellent Blasts
